                                              About me#

                                              I am a 3rd year bachelor student at Faculty of Automatic Control and Computer Science, University Politehnica of Bucharest, locally known as Facultatea de Automatică și Calculatoare (CTI), Universitatea Politehnica București.

                                              I went down this path because I liked doing things my way. Building something from the ground up gives you a kind of satisfaction unachievable by any other means.

                                              Engineering Interests#

                                              In the past, I was passionate about cryptography and chess engines. Don't get me wrong, I still enjoy these topics, but I've shifted focus a little.

                                              I am currently interested in numerical methods, electrical engineering, parsers & compilers and computer architecture; I am also somewhat intrigued by quantum computing.
